BURNING MAN

Fun Stuff

I made a Moon for Burning Man 2017 that lights up in phases, much like the moon itself. I built it with plexiglass, wood, LEDs, and *lots* of saran wrap.

For context: At Burning Man, theme camps like mine pick an identity that shapes their offering to the wider community of the playa. My camp’s mission is to “inspire a childlike sense of wonder” in our visitors, so I designed a symbol that references some of our favorite, nostalgic images (A Trip to the Moon, The Smashing Pumpkins’ “Tonight, Tonight” video, and Goodnight, Moon.)
