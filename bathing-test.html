BATHING CULTURE

Brand + Web Design

I launched a new section of the blog for Bathing Culture, a soap company. For each post of the Bath Tapes, I curated a playlist, wrote the copy, and created the album art. I also designed a dynamic, adaptable logo that lives on each album cover. The imagery embraces Bathing Culture's psychedelic, nostalgic, and idiosyncratic aesthetic: washed-out colors, unconventional type choices, and a warm vibe.
